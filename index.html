Routine & Form Guide

💡 Understanding Effort Levels (RIR)

HIGH EFFORT (1 RIR — Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri)
Stop 1 rep short of failure. The final rep should feel slow, heavy, and intense, but done with clean form. Forces strength & muscle growth.
MEDIUM EFFORT (3–4 RIR — Wed)
Stop with 3 to 4 easy reps left in the tank. Smooth, controlled speed. Focuses on mind-muscle squeeze, blood flow, and joint recovery.
LIGHT EFFORT (5+ RIR — Sat)
Very easy bodyweight movement. Keeps your daily habit active without accumulating any muscular or central nervous system fatigue.

Exercise Form Guides

1. Pull-Ups / Chin-Ups
Target: Lats & Biceps. Drive your elbows down toward your hips. Overhand grip hits upper back; underhand grip (chin-ups) hits biceps more.
2. Chest Dips
Target: Lower Chest & Triceps. Lean slightly forward (~20°) to target chest. Lower until upper arms are parallel to the floor (90° bend).
3. Deficit Push-Ups
Target: Mid Chest & Core. Use push-up handles to elevate hands. Lower down until chest dips below handle level for a deeper chest stretch.
4. DB Lateral Raises
Target: Side Shoulders (Width). Raise 8 lb dumbbells outward with elbows slightly bent. Take 3 seconds to lower for maximum burn.
5. DB Rear Delt Flyes
Target: Rear Shoulders & Upper Back. Hinge forward at the hips. Raise 8 lb dumbbells wide to the sides, driving with your back elbows to fix posture.
6. Hanging Leg Raises
Target: Lower Abs & Core. Hang from pull-up bar. Lift knees or legs upward smoothly without swinging. Tilt pelvis at top to contract abs.
7. Plank Hold
Target: Full Core Stability. Rest on forearms and toes. Keep your body in a straight line from ears to ankles. Squeeze glutes and belly button.
